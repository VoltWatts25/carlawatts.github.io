About me


I'm a student at Boise State University (BSU) with GIMM as a major. Since I was young, the idea of being able to develop a video game had been a dream of mine. I would create art of characters and monsters of a nonexistant game, but I didn't think I'd ever acutally be able to achive this goal. I would keep drawing, as that was and still is my passion, but when I finally enrolled to BSU I realised that this old dream of mine was right around the corner. I finally got the chance to work on my own mini video game, art, code, and everything. It was difficult, but I want to keep improving my coding skills to create something grand.
